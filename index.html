Experiments

Interactive experiments in linguistics and social science, implemented with magpie. These demos illustrate different methods for collecting and structuring behavioral data.

Psycholinguistic Experiments

Paradigms in pragmatics and sentence processing focusing on interpretation, acceptability, and online processing.

Forced-Choice Task

Participants choose between predefined response options to indicate interpretation preferences, here used to investigate the resolution of expressive adjectives.

Run Experiment

Referent Selection Task

Participants identify the perceived target of the scope of offensivenes of a pejorative, clicking on faces presented.

Run Experiment

Likert-Scale Rating

Participants rate the acceptability or felicity of linguistic stimuli, here applied to wh-exclamatives in context (answer options become clickable only after 10 seconds).

Run Experiment

Incongruence Detection Task

Words are presented sequentially and participants press a button when they detect an incongruent word, allowing measurement of how quickly mismatches in pejorative interpretation are identified.

Run Experiment

Self-Paced Reading (Processing Effort)

Measures online processing effort during sentence comprehension, here applied to the processing of gender-related pejoratives.

Run Experiment
